

four control bits:

00 - data
	00 - no op
	01 - pop (discard)
	10 - push
	11 - replace
01 - reserved (unary ops?) (shift? rotate?)
	//increment, decrement, negate, not, push zero, clear, shift, rotate, count...
	00 - shift
	01 - rotate
	10 - increment (only if no carry in connected)
	11 - push zero
10 - arithmetic
	00 - add
	01 - subtract
	10 - multiply (put high bits on stack)
	11 - divide (put modulus on stack)
11 - logic
	00 - or
	01 - and
	10 - xor
	11 - xand

four flags:
	00 - zero
	01 - carry / borrow out
	10 - sign
	11 - stack empty
	
	carry / borrow in
